# NCHU AI Counselor Product Context

## Product

NCHU AI Counselor is a Phase 1 student-support system for a university counselor workflow. The current repository combines a FastAPI backend and a Vite React frontend prototype for student Q&A, resource discovery, conversation history, knowledge maintenance, audit, stats, and counselor assistance.

## Audience

- Students who need clear answers, resource links, and continuity across support conversations.
- Counselors and administrators who need fast triage, knowledge-base maintenance, audit visibility, and workload summaries.
- Internal school stakeholders who value reliability, clarity, traceability, and restrained institutional presentation.

## Product Tone

- Calm, trustworthy, and work-focused.
- Clear Chinese-first operational language when building user-facing UI.
- Avoid marketing-heavy copy, playful metaphors, or decorative complexity that reduces task clarity.
- Prefer direct labels, visible status, transparent system state, and human handoff affordances.

## Design Priorities

1. Make high-frequency student and counselor workflows easy to scan and repeat.
2. Keep dense information structured with strong hierarchy, predictable navigation, and clear empty/error/loading states.
3. Use visual polish to support confidence, not to create a consumer landing-page feel.
4. Treat accessibility, responsive behavior, and readable typography as baseline requirements.
5. Preserve auditability and institutional trust in wording, state transitions, and interaction design.

## Anti-References

- Overly playful chatbot UI that hides operational state.
- Generic SaaS hero sections when the task is a working product surface.
- One-note color themes, low-contrast glass effects, and decorative backgrounds that compete with content.
- Brand imitation unless a specific `awesome-design-md` reference is explicitly selected for exploration.
